Armada Optimus Prime is the newest incarnation of the our favorite Autobot leader. Prime has 4 modes, Tractor Trailer, Robot mode, Super Robot mode, and Base mode.

-Truck Mode-
In truck mode Optimus is a futuristic truck. His truck mode sports the original colors of the original Optimus Prime. The truck measures 12 inches long and 3 and 12/16 tall. His truck mode has 6 Powerlinx points (2 on the Tractor, 4 on Trailer)

-Robot Mode-
In Robot Mode Optimus is 7 inches tall and 5 inches wide. Prime's Robot mode is by far my favorite out of all of his modes, out of all Generations. In Prime's chest, if you pull down the truck grill, he has a version of The Matrix; this is the first American designed version of Optimus Prime to have a Matrix.

-Base Mode-
The Base Mode measure 14 inches and 1/2 inches by 15 and 1/2 inches. The Base Mode has 3 Powerlinx points, 1 of which when a Minicon is place on a gun pops up. He also has a launcher on is leg that fires a small missile. The base mode also has 2 garages, 1 of them is equipped with a launcher for Minicons.

-Super Robot Mode-
Super Prime is exactly 12 inches tall, and is exactly 8 inches wide at his shoulders. This is my least favorite mode, the reason being that he fall apart most of the time. The only other real thing that I can note is that he has an LED on his left arm, that when you put his gun in his hand and press the button his gun lights up.

-Minicon-
Optimus's Minicon is named Sparkplug, he is a Lamborghini Diablo. Sparkplug is 2 inches long by 1 inch wide in car mode. In robot mode his is 2 inches tall, by 2 inches wide at the shoulders. The only other thing that is noteworthy is that he kinda looks like a futuristic Bumblebee.

-Packaging-
His package measures 14 inches long and 5 inches wide. His box looks like a cross between a Beast Machines box, and a RID box. On one panel there is a pic of Prime in super mode, and the opposite is the story of Armada. On the back of his box, is a layout of all of his modes. On the bottom of his box there is pics of other available Armada toys.

-Inserts-
Inside Primes box is a little baggy, in the baggy is his Tech Spec image, a missile, a comic book, and instructions.

-Overall-
Overall Prime is a good toy, but the fact that is Super mode lacks a back, and not having articulation in his legs subtracts form the greatness of this toy. Also the fact that the Laser function on this toy does not work correctly, and the sounds that the toy makes are really dumb cause the toy to receive an 7/10.
